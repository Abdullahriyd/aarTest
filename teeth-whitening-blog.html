Does Teeth Whitening Work Fast?

Is one hour fast enough for a glowing, bright smile? At HB Smile Dental, Dr. Steven Nguyen uses the benefits of new technology to deliver safe, effective whitening without side effects. Teeth whitening can be used in different methods to achieve its goals. It can be accomplished by using bleaching agents to reduce the stains and discoloration on tooth enamel.

It usually involves a special gel that is applied directly to the teeth, and then exposed to a special light or laser to activate the whitening process. Professional treatments are usually done in an office setting, while over-the-counter products can be used at home with varying levels of success. For best results, it is important to follow package instructions carefully to get desired results without damaging tooth enamel or causing other side effects.
